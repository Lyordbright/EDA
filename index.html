About EDA

The U.S. Economic Development Administration's mission is to lead the federal economic development agenda by promoting innovation and competitiveness, preparing American regions for growth and success in the worldwide economy.

EDA makes investments in economically distressed communities in order to create jobs for U.S. workers, promote American innovation, and accelerate long-term sustainable economic growth.

Through the Winning Money Program, we provide direct financial assistance to qualified Americans who have earned rewards through various federal initiatives.

Our Programs

Infrastructure Grants

Funding for public works and infrastructure development projects that support economic development and create jobs.

  • Road and bridge construction
  • Broadband infrastructure
  • Water and sewer systems
  • Energy efficiency projects
Innovation Grants

Support for innovation and entrepreneurship to create a vibrant economic ecosystem in communities nationwide.

  • Research and development
  • Technology commercialization
  • Startup incubation
  • Workforce development
Winning Money Program

Direct financial assistance to Americans who have qualified for rewards through federal programs and initiatives.

  • Simple eligibility verification
  • Fast disbursement of funds
  • Dedicated support team
  • Multiple payment options

Eligibility Requirements
  • U.S. Citizen or legal resident
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • At least 18 years of age
  • Proof of address
  • Bank account for direct deposit (optional)
